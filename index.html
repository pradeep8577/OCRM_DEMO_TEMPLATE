Mission Control: Managing Reports for Safer Communities

The Crime Report Management Application is to facilitate the efficient reporting, tracking, and management of crime incidents.

Incident Reporting
Allows to log details of incidents, including location, date, time, and involved parties.
Analytics and reporting
Enable the generation of statistical reports based on different parameters (crime type, location, time).
Case Management
Provide a system for creating and managing cases related to reported incidents.
Status History
Maintain a comprehensive status history log for each report.
